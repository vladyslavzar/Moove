Moove Freight Delivery (MFD)

We created MFD to deliver goods from A to B in the
most reliable, cheap and safe way. We build and use
special hubs in highways, setting new standards
in how trucks will move freight.

How it works

01

Truck with a driver from carrier
comes to hub close to point A.

02

Trailer from the carrier's truck couples
to Moove autonomous truck.

03

Moove truck is heading to hub B
in autonomous mode.

04

In end-point hub trailer uncouples from Moove
self-driving truck and couples to carrier’s truck.

05

Carrier’s truck with a driver
is heading to final destination.
